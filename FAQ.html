Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a variety of pet sitting services, including dog walking, cat sitting, overnight pet care, and more. You can find a detailed description of our services on our Services page.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes, we are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. This means that we have insurance coverage in case of any accidents or injuries, and we are bonded to guarantee financial responsibility.

How do I book a pet sitting service?

You can easily book a service by contacting us through our Contact Us page. We'll discuss your pet's needs and availability to schedule a pet sitting visit.
