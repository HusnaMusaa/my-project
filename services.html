Our Services

We offer a variety of pet sitting services to cater to your pet's needs. Whether you need someone to walk your dog, feed your cat, or check in on your small pet, we can help.

Dog Walking & Playtime

We provide daily walks for your furry friend, ensuring they get the exercise and fresh air they need. We can tailor the walk to your dog's energy level and needs, whether it's a brisk walk in the park or a leisurely stroll around the neighborhood.

Cat Sitting & Feeding

Don't worry about your feline friend while you're away. We'll visit your cat daily to provide fresh food, water, and litter box cleaning. We can also play with them and offer them some TLC.

Overnight Pet Care

For added peace of mind, we offer overnight pet sitting services. We'll stay at your home to provide companionship and care for your pet throughout the night.
